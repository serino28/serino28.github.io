    EACL 2026 ‚Äî Rabat, Morocco

    EMNLP 2025 (Industry Track) ‚Äî Suzhou, China

    We propose SFAL, a novel evaluation framework that measures how well LLM-generated feature explanations align with actual feature behavior. Unlike costly simulation-based methods, SFAL uses embedding-based semantic similarity for fast, scalable assessment of auto-interpretability quality.

    ECML-PKDD 2025 ‚Äî Porto, Portugal
